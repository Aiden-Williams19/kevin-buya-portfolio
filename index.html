About

KB
Cape Town, South Africa

I don't make content for the sake of making content. Every frame, every cut, every second of footage has to earn its place.

Based in Cape Town, I've spent years building a practice that spans filmmaking, photography, on-camera performance, and creative direction. I'm not a specialist in one thing — I'm fluent across the entire production chain, from concept to final export.

That means when you work with me, you're not coordinating between six different people. You get one creative who understands the full picture — someone who can direct a shoot, step in front of the camera, and cut the final edit with the same level of care.

I've shot brand partnerships, personal films, event coverage, and everything in between. My approach is the same every time: make it real, make it look incredible, and make sure it actually says something.
