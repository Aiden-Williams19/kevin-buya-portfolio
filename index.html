About Me

Cape Town, South Africa

I don't make content for the sake of making content. Every frame, every cut, every second of footage has to earn its place.

I'm a multi-disciplinary creative with experience across video editing, content creation, photography, acting, presenting, music performance, voice-over, podcasts, and graphic design. That range isn't accidental — it's what makes my work different.

When you work with me, you're not coordinating between six different people. You get one creative who understands the full picture — someone who can direct a shoot, step in front of the camera, and cut the final edit with the same level of care.

I've shot brand partnerships, personal films, event coverage, matric dance highlights, music videos, and everything in between. My approach is the same every time: make it real, make it look incredible, and make sure it actually says something.
