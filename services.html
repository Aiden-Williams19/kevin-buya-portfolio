Services

What I
Bring to the Table

I work across the full creative spectrum. Here's where I add the most value — and how we can work together.

01

Video Production & Editing

From concept to color grade — brand films, social content, short films, event recaps, music videos. I handle the full pipeline or plug into your existing production.

Whether you need a 15-second Instagram reel or a full short film, I bring the same level of craft and attention. I shoot, I edit, I deliver — ready to publish.

What this covers

  • Brand & commercial video
  • Short films & narrative
  • Social media content (Reels, TikTok, YouTube)
  • Event coverage & highlight reels
  • Music videos & BTS
  • Post-production, color grading & sound
  • Motion graphics & titles
02

Photography

Self-taught with a natural eye. I shoot weddings, events, portraits, products, sports, and editorial — always prioritizing authentic and empathetic moments over posed perfection.

I believe the best photos happen when people forget there's a camera in the room. That's the kind of work I make.

What this covers

  • Weddings & engagements
  • Events & celebrations
  • Portraits & headshots
  • Product photography
  • Sports & action
  • Editorial & lifestyle
  • Bridal showers & graduations
03

On-Camera Talent

Acting, presenting, hosting — I'm as comfortable in front of the lens as I am behind it. I've done national commercials, live co-hosting, performance pieces, and voice-over work.

If you need someone who can deliver natural, authentic on-camera presence — whether it's scripted or improvised — I'm your person.

What this covers

  • Commercial acting
  • Event hosting & MC
  • Presenting & co-hosting
  • Voice-over & podcast
  • Music performance
  • Improvisation & live work
04

Creative Direction

The connective tissue between all of the above. I help brands and individuals figure out what their story actually is — then build the visual world to tell it.

This is for projects where you need someone to think holistically about the creative. Not just execution — strategy, concept, and direction.

What this covers

  • Concept development
  • Art direction
  • Brand content strategy
  • Campaign ideation
  • Visual identity guidance
  • Multi-platform content planning
05

Graphic Design

Social media graphics, thumbnails, brand assets, flyers, and digital design. I handle visual design work that complements the bigger creative picture.

What this covers

  • Social media graphics
  • Thumbnails & covers
  • Brand collateral
  • Flyers & promotional material
  • Digital design assets
Process

How I Work

Simple, clear, no drama. Here's how every project goes.

01

Brief & Align

We talk. I listen. We get clear on what you actually need — not just what you think you need. This is where we set expectations, timelines, and budgets.

02

Concept & Plan

I come back with a direction. Mood, tone, format, timeline, budget — all mapped out before we shoot a single frame or take a single photo.

03

Produce & Deliver

I execute. You review. We refine. Final assets delivered ready to publish, broadcast, or frame on your wall. No endless back-and-forth.

Ready to start?

Tell me about your project and I'll come back with a plan and a quote. No pressure, no hard sell.
